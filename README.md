## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes program performance using participant data to understand enrollment trends, completion rates, and exit outcomes from 2021 to 2026.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
The goal is to answer:
- Are participants completing the program?
- What are the most common exit outcomes?
- Are there differences in outcomes across groups?
- Are there retention challenges?

---

## 📁 Dataset
Two datasets were used:
- Enrollment data (participant demographics and move-in dates)
- Exit status data (program outcomes)

Both datasets were combined using a common participant ID.

---

## 📊 Analysis & Insights

### Exit Outcomes
- Quit is the most common exit (~39%)
- Completion rate is about ~28%
👉 Indicates strong retention challenges

---

### Completion Rate by Race
- Asian: ~27.8% (highest)
- White & Hispanic: ~26%
- Black: ~25%
👉 Shows small differences across groups

---

### Enrollment Trends
- Enrollment changes over time with noticeable fluctuations
👉 Indicates inconsistent intake patterns

---

## 🧾 Conclusion
Although enrollment remains steady, completion rates are relatively low (~28%). A large number of participants exit early, mainly due to quitting (~39%).

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Improve retention strategies to reduce quit rates  
- Provide early support for at-risk participants  
- Monitor completion rate as a key performance metric
